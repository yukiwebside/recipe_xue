Recipe

Eggs

Benedict

By Yu Xue

Health and Yummy!

  • Hollandaise Sauce

    • Butter 150g
    • Eggs 3
    • Lemon half
    • Salt/Pepper
    1. Melt butter on lowheat to make clarified butter.
    2. Boil small amount of water in a small pot.
    3. Then turn down the heat after boiled to remain simmering.
    4. Separate egg yolks from egg whites and put into a container.
    5. Then set the container on top of the simmering pot, squeeze half a lemon in, and beat the egg yolks as fas as you can to achieve a thickening texture.
    6. Remove container from pot immediately when reached the texture.
    7. Little by little add clarified butter and mix gently. Add appropriate salt and pepper.

  • Eggs

    • English muffins 1/split in half
    • Hollandaise Sauce 80g
    • Spinach
    • Bacon/Smoked Salmon 2slices
    • Eggs 2
    • Salt/Pepper
    • Butter
    • White Vinegar(optional)
    1. Saut'e Spanach leaves with butter.
    2. Roast muffins with butter in a separatepan until crispy and golden brown.
    3. Boil a pot of water with white vinegar(3:1).
    4. Swirl the water. Poach the eggs in the center of the swirl when water boils.
    5. Boil for 4-5 minutes and take out immediately into a nice bath.
    6. Sear bacon or take out two pieces of salmon.
    7. Dren the plate in the order of muffins, bacon/salmon, spinach, eggs, Hollandaise sauce.
